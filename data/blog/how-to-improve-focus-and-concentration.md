Read a short passage, close the book, and recount it in detail. Begin with simple stories and aim for accuracy. This sharpens both focus and memory.

## Extra Tips to Stay on Track

Try these bonus tricks to keep distractions at bay:

- **Pomodoro Technique**: Work for 25 minutes, then break for 5. It keeps you fresh and focused.
- **Mindfulness Moments**: Spend 10 minutes breathing deeply. It trains your brain to ignore noise.
- **One Task at a Time**: Multitasking kills focusâ€”stick to one thing until it's done.
- **Clear Your Space**: Silence notifications and tidy up. A clean setup boosts concentration.
- **Rest and Recharge**: Fix your sleep and eat well. A healthy body fuels a focused mind.
